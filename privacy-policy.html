1. Privacy Policy

Horizonical Export Imports ("we", "us", "our") respects the privacy of everyone who visits this website or contacts us to enquire about our products. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and what choices you have.

What data we collect

When you use our enquiry form, we collect:

  • Your name and company name
  • Your email address
  • Details of the product and quantity you're enquiring about, and any other information you choose to include in your message

We do not require account creation, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

How we use your data

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and prepare quotations
  • Communicate with you about an order, sample, or shipment
  • Maintain records required for export documentation and compliance

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. We may share information with logistics partners, customs brokers, or inspection agencies strictly where necessary to fulfil an order you've placed with us.

Cookies & analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site (e.g. which pages are viewed, general location, and device type). Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this information anonymously in aggregate. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or by adjusting your browser's cookie settings.

We do not use cookies for advertising or retargeting.

Your rights

If you are located in the UK, EU, or another jurisdiction with data protection laws that grant you rights over your personal data, you may have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data, where we are not legally required to retain it (e.g. for export/customs records)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@horizonical.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Data retention

We retain enquiry and order-related data for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry, fulfil an order, and meet our legal and export-documentation obligations. Where data is no longer needed for these purposes, we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymise it.
